Frequently Asked Questions

Transparent answers to common founder questions.

How long does the acquisition process take?

From initial contact to closing, typically 4–8 weeks depending on complexity and due diligence.

Do you require exclusivity?

We prefer it during active due diligence (2‑3 weeks), but we understand you might be talking to other buyers.

What is your average valuation multiple?

We typically pay 3x to 6x annual profit, based on growth, retention, and market.

Will I need to stay on after the sale?

Optional – we can offer a transition period or a clean exit as you prefer.
